# IndexNow

## Summary

IndexNow is a search engine notification protocol considered for MatchWalls, especially for [[wiki/Entities/bing|Bing]] and related discovery.

## Key points

- Not active in the current evidence.
- `indexnow.txt` is documented as 404.
- The blocker is verifiable root key hosting for `www.matchwalls.com`.
- No URL submissions should be made until the key and URL policy are resolved.
