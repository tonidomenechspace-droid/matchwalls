## Summary

Source summary for `raw/auditoria-seo-geo-matchwalls/source-validation-MW-ENTITY-FACTUAL-SOURCE-VALIDATION-015B-2026-07-05.md`. It validates which entity claims can be used publicly or in schema.

## Key points

- Official entity source coverage is incomplete.
- Safe fields: brand name, domain, online store type, safe offer, priority languages, and general topics.
- Blocked fields: legal name, tax ID, address, phone, email, `sameAs`, founding date, Barcelona claim, manufacturing, certifications, reviews, shipping, guarantees.
