# Informe Machtwell Erismann business plan

## Summary

Source summary for the local folder `raw/informe_machtwell/`. The folder contains confidential [[wiki/Entities/matchwalls|MatchWalls]] business-plan deliverables prepared for [[wiki/Entities/erismann|Erismann]], including a multilingual HTML presentation and Excel workbooks in ES, EN, and DE.

This source should be treated as confidential M&A / business development material. The raw folder is ignored by Git and this wiki page intentionally avoids copying sensitive identifiers, exact legal details, and detailed financial tables.

## Key points

- The deliverable frames MatchWalls as a digital growth and acquisition opportunity for Erismann.
- The material includes a business plan, execution timeline, multi-market pricing, operational KPIs, cost structure, P&L, M&A deal structure, digital strategy, and asset inventory.
- The plan connects ecommerce operations with [[wiki/Concepts/seo|SEO]], [[wiki/Concepts/geo|GEO]], [[wiki/Concepts/aeo|AEO]], paid media, reporting, technology upgrades, and business transferability.
- The Excel files contain nine sheets in each language version.
- The HTML deliverable is branded as `Matchwalls + WECO = Erismann Digital Growth`.
