# Indexability

## Summary

Indexability is the ability of MatchWalls URLs to be discoverable, crawlable, canonical, and eligible for indexing.

## Key points

- Current evidence shows sitemap and robots are accessible.
- IndexNow is not active.
- Broad validation is blocked until infrastructure uncertainty is resolved.
