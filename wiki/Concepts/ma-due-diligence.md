# M&A due diligence

## Summary

M&A due diligence is the process of evaluating MatchWalls as an investable or acquirable business, including assets, financial assumptions, operational dependencies, transferability, risks, and growth plan.

## Key points

- The Erismann business-plan source includes deal structure, governance, P&L, asset inventory, digital strategy, and operational assumptions.
- The most important distinction is between standard third-party tools and transferable proprietary value.
- [[wiki/Entities/hub-matchwalls|Hub MatchWalls]] can strengthen the due-diligence narrative if it becomes a documented operational layer rather than only an idea.
