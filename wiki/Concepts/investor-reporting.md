# Investor reporting

## Summary

Investor reporting is the packaged communication of MatchWalls performance, risks, opportunities, assets, and roadmap for a buyer or investor audience.

## Key points

- It should connect financial evidence, traffic evidence, Shopify operations, digital strategy, and transferable assets.
- It should present SEO/GEO/SEM as a growth roadmap, not as panic or unmanaged technical debt.
- It should distinguish standard SaaS tooling from proprietary data, processes, automations, and operational know-how.
