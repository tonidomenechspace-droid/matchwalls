# Business transferability

## Summary

Business transferability is the degree to which MatchWalls can be understood, operated, and valued by another owner without depending only on undocumented personal knowledge.

## Key points

- A Shopify store alone is less differentiated than a documented operating system.
- [[wiki/Entities/hub-matchwalls|Hub MatchWalls]] can add value if it centralizes data, processes, reporting, integrations, and automations.
- Transferable value should distinguish standard third-party apps from proprietary workflows, clean data, and operational know-how.
