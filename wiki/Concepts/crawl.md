# Crawl

## Summary

Crawl refers to public or bot-like requests used to validate how MatchWalls is seen by search engines and AI systems.

## Key points

- Broad public crawls are blocked by the current infrastructure hold.
- Lightweight checks are acceptable when scoped and documented.
- The audit distinguishes crawler failures from editorial SEO failures.
