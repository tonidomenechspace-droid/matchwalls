# Entity facts

## Summary

Entity facts are verified claims that can be used to describe MatchWalls in copy, schema, AI-facing content, and reports.

## Key points

- Safe facts are intentionally conservative.
- Strong company claims remain blocked until source validation is complete.
- Entity facts should drive schema and GEO/AEO copy.
